PB & J Recipe

Description

The ultimate peanut butter and jelly Sandwich can be yours! All you have to do is follow this handy dandy recipe.

Prepare for an explosion of flavor in your mouth!

Ingredients:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Get two slices of bread.
  2. Step 2: With a butter knife, spread peanut butter evenly over one side of bread.
  3. Step 3: Next, spread a thin layer of jelly on the other slicea of bread.
  4. Step 4: Bring the two pieces of bread together.
  5. Step 5: Slice diagonally, or vertically into two symmetrical halves.
  6. Step 6: Enjoy with a glass of skim milk!
