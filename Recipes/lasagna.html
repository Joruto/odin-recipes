Lasagna Recipe

Description

Enjoy this delicious homemade lasanga recipe from my Great Grandmother's cookbook!

In just 30 minutes you'll be enjoying your very own slice of this authentic italian lasagna!

Ingredients:

  • Lasanga
  • Shredded Parmesian Cheese
  • Basil
  • Tomatoes
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Red Pepper (Optional)

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Get into your car.
  2. Step 2: Drive to Costco.
  3. Step 3: Purchase Kirkland Lasanga.
  4. Step 4: Drive home.
  5. Step 5: Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  6. Step 6: Cook lasagna for 60 minutes.
  7. Step 7: Read a book while you wait, or take a bath.
  8. Step 8: Get oven mitt from drawer.
  9. Step 9: Carefully retrive lasanga from oven. Warning: Contents will be hot!
  10. Step 10: Enjoy!
